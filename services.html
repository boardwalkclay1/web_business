Custom Integrations (Explained)

Custom integrations connect your website or business system to other tools you use. This removes manual work and automates your operations.

Examples:

  • Connecting your website to QuickBooks
  • Connecting booking forms to Google Calendar
  • Connecting your CRM to email or SMS systems
  • Connecting your store to shipping carriers

Microcontroller Services (Explained)

Microcontrollers are tiny computers that control physical devices. We use them to automate real‑world tasks for businesses.

Examples:

  • Smart inventory sensors
  • Automated door locks
  • Temperature or humidity monitoring
  • Motion‑activated lighting
  • BLE mesh networks for warehouses
